A fast, client-side dictionary browser. Pick a search mode (starts-with or
ends-with), type a few letters, and optionally click letters in the keyboard
map to **require** them (green) or **forbid** them (red). Every word in the
~84k engmix English wordlist is filtered live in your browser — no server, no
tracking.

Wordlist: engmix (public-domain mixed English wordlist), shipped as a single
~230 KB compressed text file.
