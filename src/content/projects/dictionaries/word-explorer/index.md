A fast, client-side dictionary browser. Pick a search mode (starts-with or
ends-with), type a few letters, and optionally click letters in the keyboard
map to **require** them (green) or **forbid** them (red). Every word in the
~380k merged English wordlist is filtered live in your browser — no server, no
tracking.

Wordlist: a merge of [dwyl/english-words](https://github.com/dwyl/english-words)
(classic English + inflections), [hermitdave/FrequencyWords](https://github.com/hermitdave/FrequencyWords)
en_50k (modern OpenSubtitles vocabulary), [first20hours/google-10000-english](https://github.com/first20hours/google-10000-english),
and a small hand-curated supplement of recent web/tech terms.
